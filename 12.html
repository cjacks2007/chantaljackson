Retail Sales Analysis (SQL Queries)

This project simulates a retail sales database to perform detailed business analysis using SQL. I built the schema and wrote queries to uncover key business insights such as revenue trends, top-performing products, and high-value customers.

Key Features

  1. Monthly revenue tracking
  2. Top 5 products by units sold
  3. Regional revenue breakdown
  4. Customer lifetime value.
