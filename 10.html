Personal Finance Tracker

This Excel project helps individuals monitor and manage their finances over a year. The tracker includes income sources (salary, freelance) and expense categories (rent, groceries, utilities, etc.). It provides a basis for creating dashboards that visualize cash flow, budgeting trends, and spending patterns.

Key Features

  1. Monthly income vs expenses chart
  2. Monthly Savings Trend: Visualize how much is saved (or overspent) over time
  3. Total Expense by Category: Identify where most of the money goes.
  4. Overall Income vs Expense Distribution: Snapshot of your financial health.
  • Tools: Excel formulas (SUMIFS, IF, etc.), Pivot Tables for monthly summaries, Charts for category breakdowns
