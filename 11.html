Sales Performance Dashboard

In this Excel project, a comprehensive sales dashboard was created to track and analyze the monthly sales performance of an electronics store. Using generated sales data covering one year, the dashboard allows detailed analysis by product category, sales region, and revenue trends.

Key Features

  1. Interactive Pivot Tables and Charts: Quickly summarize key metrics like units sold and revenue.
  2. Slicers: Allow interactive filtering by product category, region, and dates.
  3. Dynamic Visualization: Employ conditional formatting to highlight trends and exceptional performance.
  • Skills Demonstrated: Data management and cleaning, Advanced Excel pivot tables, Interactive slicers and charts creation, Revenue and sales performance analysis
