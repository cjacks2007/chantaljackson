Customer Credit Risk Analysis

I conducted an in-depth credit risk analysis to identify patterns and predictors of loan default risks among customers. Using Power BI, I explored how factors like loan purpose, account balances, employment duration, age, gender, marital status, and housing status relate to credit risk levels. I built interactive dashboards featuring bar charts, scatter plots, box plots, and heat maps to reveal actionable insights. This project sharpened my skills in data visualization, business intelligence storytelling, and risk modeling for financial services.
