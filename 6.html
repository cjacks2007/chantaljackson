Hospital Management Database Design

I developed a comprehensive relational database model to streamline hospital operations, covering critical domains like patients, doctors, medications, prescriptions, staff, departments, and room assignments. The logical model ensures efficient handling of patient care, staff management, medication tracking, and departmental organization. This project showcases my ability to design complex, multi-entity data systems that support healthcare delivery, regulatory compliance, and hospital administration needs.

  • Problem: Hospitals need an organized way to manage patients, staff, medication, and operations..
  • Solution: Designed a normalized, relational database structure for seamless information management.
  • Impact: Enables better patient care tracking, streamlined staff operations, and improved hospital efficiency.
