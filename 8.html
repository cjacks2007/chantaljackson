US Job Market Trend Analysis with Python

This project explores trends in the US tech job market using a simulated dataset of job postings. The analysis focuses on demand, salary levels, and geographic distribution of roles. Python libraries like pandas, matplotlib, and seaborn were used to process and visualize the data.

Key Features

  1. Most in-demand job titles in the US tech market
  2. Salary analysis across roles like ML Engineer, AI Specialist, Data Analyst
  3. Geographic distribution of job availability (NY, SF, Austin, etc.)
  4. Weekly posting trends for market momentum
  • Tools: Python, pandas, seaborn, matplotlib
